# Aggressive Information

## What is Aggressive Sea of Thieves?

**Aggressive** is a powerful Sea of Thieves menu designed to give players greater control and precision during gameplay. It offers a smooth, easy-to-use interface with advanced visual tools, aiming assistance, and quality-of-life utilities that enhance both combat and exploration. With deep customization options and reliable performance, Aggressive is built for players who want consistency, clarity, and an edge on every voyage.
